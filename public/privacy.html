Data & Privacy

Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and your rights under UK GDPR.

Last updated: June 9, 2026

americanvisaguide.com is a community resource operated by Erin Dyer Saxon.

What data we collect

1. Timeline submissions via FillOut

When you submit your visa timeline via the FillOut form, you provide dates and milestones. This data is stored in Supabase and used to generate aggregate statistics.

  • Individual submissions are not publicly displayed
  • Only aggregated trends and anonymised statistics are shown
  • FillOut privacy: https://www.fillout.com/privacy

2. Analytics via Microsoft Clarity

This site uses Microsoft Clarity to understand visitor interactions. Clarity may record session replays and heatmaps.

  • No personal data is intentionally included
  • Session data is aggregated and anonymised
  • Clarity privacy: https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement

3. Browser-stored worksheet data

Worksheets store inputs in your browser tab memory only — never transmitted to any server, lost on tab close.

4. Cookies

No first-party cookies. Microsoft Clarity may set third-party cookies. No advertising or tracking cookies.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of submitted timelines
  • Object to processing

Contact: AmericanVisaGuideInfo@gmail.com

Data retention

Timeline submissions are retained indefinitely for statistics accuracy. Deletion requests will remove your submission and recalculate aggregates.

Third-party services

  • Supabase: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • FillOut: https://www.fillout.com/privacy
  • Microsoft Clarity: https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement
  • Ko-fi: https://more.ko-fi.com/privacy

Not legal advice

This site provides general immigration information based on official government sources and community experience. Nothing constitutes legal advice. For legal advice, consult a qualified immigration attorney.
