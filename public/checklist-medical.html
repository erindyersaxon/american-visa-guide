Medical Day Checklist

Your medical and document check both happen on the same day.

🚨 Critical: You must attend the US Embassy for document check on the same day as your medical examination. Do not book them on separate days. The embassy doc check window is 11:45 AM to 1:00 PM — go even if you are running late.

Bottom line: If you complete medical, receive pink slip, AND complete embassy document check on the same day, you are fully cleared for interview.
