Medical Day Checklist

Your medical and document check both happen on the same day.

🚨 Critical: You must attend the US Embassy for document check on the same day as your medical examination. Do not book them on separate days. The embassy doc check window is 11:45 AM to 1:00 PM — go even if you are running late.

1. What to bring — medical day

Category Required Notes
Identity ✓ Passport (original, not copy). Interview letter (printed from email).
Forms & Documents ✓ DS-260 confirmation (printed from CEAC), IV18a questionnaire (printed). ACRO police certificate (original). Bring all as printed documents, NOT on phone.
Medical Records ✓ Vaccination records (original booklet). GP summary (if requested or helpful, optional but recommended).
Personal Items * Glasses or contact lenses if worn (required for exam). Debit/credit card for payment (preferred method).

For a complete breakdown of all document requirements at each stage (I-130, NVC, Medical, Interview) with detailed notes on formats and translations, see Master Document Checklist.
