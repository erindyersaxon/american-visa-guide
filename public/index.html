About this site

Over 200 people going through US Embassy London have shared their milestone dates. The tracker shows real wait times, real outcomes, and when the next interview letter drop is likely to happen.

The London process guide covers every stage from filing the I-130 to picking up your passport. Written in plain language, based on what the embassy actually does.

Built by Erin, who went through the London process. Independent, volunteer-run, and free. Not affiliated with USCIS, the US Embassy, or any legal service.
