What this site is

American Visa Guide exists to fill the gap between the official USCIS guidance (thorough but dense) and scattered forum posts (specific but hard to trust). It's focused entirely on the US Embassy London consular process for family-based immigrant visas.

Two things: a community data tracker showing real timelines from real applicants, and a plain-language guide to every stage of the process. Both are built on what people in the community have actually experienced.

Not legal advice

This site is independent and volunteer-run. It is not affiliated with USCIS, the US Department of State, or the US Embassy London. Nothing here constitutes legal advice.

If your case is complex — waivers, prior removals, unusual circumstances — please consult a qualified immigration attorney. This site is most useful to people in a relatively straightforward family-based immigrant visa case applying through London.
