4. Document categories at NVC stage

Category Required Notes
Civil (Identity) ✓ Birth certificates for all applicants (certified copies). Marriage certificate (certified). If previously married, all divorce decrees or death certificates (certified).
Translation ✓ All non-English documents must be translated. Certified translation required if USCIS deems it necessary.
Financial ✓ Sponsor: IRS tax transcript, employment letter, financial evidence. Joint sponsor: Same requirements plus I-864A. See Section 3 for detailed list.
Forms ✓ DS-260 (one per applicant, submitted in CEAC). I-864 (wet-signed by sponsor, required for all). I-864A if joint sponsor.
Police ✓ ACRO certificate (UK) for each applicant 16+ or who lived abroad 6+ months. See Section 5 for details.
Supporting * Passport biographic page, military records, adoption documents, name change deed (if applicable). See Section 6.
Medical — Medical exam not required at NVC stage. Scheduled for Medical Day closer to interview date.

For a complete breakdown of all document requirements at each stage (I-130, NVC, Medical, Interview) with detailed notes on formats and translations, see Master Document Checklist.

5. Police certificates (ACRO for UK)

ACRO (UK):

  • Standard: £68 / up to 20 working days
  • Premium: £121 / up to 2 working days
  • Website: acro.police.uk
  • Validity: 12 months for embassy purposes
