NVC Document Checklist

What to upload to CEAC for Documentary Qualification

Checkboxes can be ticked in your browser. Your progress is not saved when you close or refresh the tab.

Before you start

Both fees must show PAID before you can upload documents

Uploading before fees clear is a common mistake. Wait for confirmation.

Financial documents — petitioner

Income threshold

Petitioner must meet 125% of US Federal Poverty Guidelines based on household size. If income is insufficient, irregular, asset-heavy, or earned abroad — a joint sponsor is required.

Financial documents — joint sponsor (if required)

London-specific risk

Asset-only or borderline income cases frequently result in 221(g) delays at London. If there is any doubt, secure a joint sponsor early — before the interview, not at it.

Civil documents — beneficiary

PCC placeholder warning

If you are using a PCC placeholder while waiting for the actual certificate, you MUST replace it with the real certificate by interview day or risk a 221(g) hold.

Top failure points

Uploading documents to the wrong CEAC category
Missing police certificates for countries lived in for 6+ months since age 16
Incomplete financial evidence — assuming assets are sufficient without a joint sponsor
Submitting before fully ready — every RFE adds approximately 2 weeks
I-864 digitally signed instead of wet signed — automatic rejection
PCC placeholder not replaced with actual certificate before interview day
