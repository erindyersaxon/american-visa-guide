Moving to the USA Workbook

A complete immigration and relocation workbook across three tabs: a London CR1/IR1 tracker covering every stage from USCIS through to your green card, a savings & expenses tracker, and a moving boxes inventory. Download your own copy to track your progress.

Created by Nikki, a brilliant mod on the Track My Visa server going through the US Embassy London process, who built this resource to share with everyone who needs it.
⬇ Download workbook (.xlsx) 📋 Copy to Google Sheets Download the Excel file or make your own Google Sheets copy — fill it in as you go through the process.

PETITIONER U.S. DOMICILE?
If the petitioner lives outside the U.S., you need to prove intent to re-establish U.S. domicile. This means showing that you are actively breaking ties with the UK to move back to the U.S. You MUST also upload a statement of intent. Failure to show strong ties to the U.S. will likely result in a 221(g).
