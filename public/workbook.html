Moving to the USA Workbook

This workbook was created by Nikki, a brilliant member of the Track My Visa community going through the US Embassy London process, who built it to share with everyone going through this journey. Thank you, Nikki.

What's included

Practical planning worksheets designed for new permanent residents. This workbook covers:

  • Banking and credit building
  • Budget planning and cost of living
  • US tax considerations and filing
  • Healthcare and insurance navigation
  • Housing search and move planning
  • Employment and licensing requirements
  • First-year expenses and timeline
