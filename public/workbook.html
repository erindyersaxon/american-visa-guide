Immediately after your medical, head to the U.S. Embassy at 33 Nine Elms Ln, London SW11 7US. Check in at the "Visa Services" tent (Visa Passport & Consular Door). Go through airport-style security. SUBMIT: Passport, medical receipt, police certificate, civil documents, DS-260 confirmation, fingerprint scan. Receive sealed envelope labelled "Do Not Tamper" for interview day.
