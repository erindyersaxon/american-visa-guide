Moving to the USA Workbook

Complete immigration and relocation tracker — from USCIS through to settling in the US.

✨ Created by Nikki, a community member who went through the US Embassy London process and built this resource to share with everyone who needs it.

A complete checklist covering every stage of the visa process and your relocation — USCIS through to your first weeks in the US. Download your own copy and fill it in as you go. Nothing is stored on this site.

📌 Your data privacy: The workbook is a personal tracking tool. Your data stays on your own device — it is not connected to americanvisaguide.com. When you download the Excel file or copy to Google Sheets, you are the only person who can see it.
💡 How to use

Download the Excel version if you prefer to work offline or share locally. Copy to Google Sheets if you want to access it from multiple devices or share with your partner. Fill in dates as you reach each milestone. Add notes in the Notes column to track what you learned or what you'd do differently — this helps the community and you when you reference it later.
