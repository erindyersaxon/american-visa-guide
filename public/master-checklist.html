Civil & Identity Documents

Document I-130 NVC Medical Interview Notes
Passport (Beneficiary) ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ Original required at all stages. Biographic page for NVC.
Birth Certificate (Beneficiary) ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ Certified copy. If UK-issued, provide translation if not in English.
Birth Certificate (Petitioner) ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ Certified copy. Establishes petitioner identity.
Marriage Certificate ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ Certified copy. Required for all spouse petitions at all stages.
Divorce Decrees / Death Certificates ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ For any prior marriage. Required at all stages if either party previously married.
Name Change Deed * * * * Required at all stages if name changed (marriage, deed poll, etc.)
Adoption Documents * * * * Required at all stages if applicable (IR2/CR2 children).

Financial Documents (I-864 Affidavit of Support)

All financial documents are for NVC stage and beyond. Primary sponsor + any joint sponsors must each complete I-864. Additional US domicile and public charge evidence required for medical and interview.

Document I-130 NVC Medical Interview Notes
I-864 (Affidavit of Support) — ✓ ✓ ✓ Wet-signed (not digital). Each sponsor completes separately.
I-864A (Joint Sponsor / Household Member) — * * * Required for any household member aged 18+ contributing income to support beneficiary.
IRS Transcript (All Sponsors) — ✓ ✓ ✓ Via IRS.gov with ID.me. Required for last 3 years minimum for all sponsors.
Tax Return Complete (All Sponsors) — ✓ ✓ ✓ Full return with all schedules if self-employed (C/E/F/SE). Last 3 years.
Employment Letter (Current Job) — ✓ ✓ ✓ Confirming employment, job title, and income for all sponsors.
US W2s and/or 1099s — ✓ ✓ ✓ Last 3 years for all sponsors. Establishes US employment history.
US Bank Statements * ✓ ✓ ✓ Recent statements showing US domicile and financial capacity. 3-6 months recommended.
US Lease or Mortgage/Deed — ✓ ✓ ✓ Proof of US domicile in sponsor's name or joint names (if married).
US Household Utility Bills — ✓ ✓ ✓ Recent bills (electricity, gas, water) showing US address. Last 3 months.
Business Bank Statements (12 months) — * * * If self-employed only. Shows business viability and cash flow.
Business Registration / EIN — * * * Proof of business establishment if self-employed.
Intent to Re-Domicile (If Applicable) — * * * For US citizens living abroad: moving contract, job contract, job applications, school/childcare inquiries, rental property contracts.
Public Charge Evidence — ✓ ✓ ✓ Evidence of US housing, US healthcare plan (employer, private, ACA marketplace), and beneficiary's professional qualifications/evidence of ability to work in US.

Relationship & Supporting Evidence

Most relationship evidence is submitted with I-130. For spouse cases, this is especially critical.

Document I-130 NVC Medical Interview Notes
Relationship Timeline ✓ — — — Narrative: how you met, key milestones, cohabitation start.
Photos (Wedding & Together) ✓ — — — Multiple dated photos with locations. Essential for spouse cases.
Travel Records ✓ — — — Evidence of joint travel. Booking confirmations, hotel receipts.
Communication Logs ✓ — — — Emails, text messages, call logs over time.
Joint Financial Accounts * — — — Bank statements with both names. Strong evidence if available; not required if spouses live in different countries.
Joint Lease or Mortgage * — — — Proof of shared residence with both names. Strong evidence if available; not required if spouses live in different countries.
Insurance Policies ✓ — — — Life, health, auto insurance naming spouse as beneficiary.
Affidavits from Friends/Family * — — — Notarized statements. Helpful if evidence sparse.

Foreign Police Certificate — * * * For any country lived 12+ months after age 16 and/or where beneficiary is a citizen. Original required.
Military Records — * * * If applicable (military service history).
Court / Prison Records — * * * If applicable (any criminal history or court proceedings).

Medical & Vaccination Documents

Document I-130 NVC Medical Interview Notes
Vaccination Records — — ✓ — Required for all beneficiaries. Original booklet/records. Bring to medical exam.
GP Summary (Medical History) — — ✓ — Required for all beneficiaries. Summary of medical history from GP.
Additional Medical Information — — * — If applicable (documentation for complex chronic conditions).
Glasses/Contact Lenses — — * — If worn regularly. Required at medical exam.
Pink Medical Checklist Slip — — ✓ ✓ Received from medical exam. REQUIRED to bring to embassy doc check and interview. Proves medical completed.

Passport Photos & Biographic Documents

Document I-130 NVC Medical Interview Notes
US-Style 2x2" Passport Photo — — ✓ ✓ 1 per beneficiary. Submitted at embassy document check. Used for sealed interview packet.
Passport Biographic Page — ✓ — — Photocopy for NVC upload.
Photocopies of All Civil Documents — — ✓ ✓ Photocopies of birth certificates, marriage certificates, divorce decrees, passports. Bring originals and copies.

Forms & Applications

Document I-130 NVC Medical Interview Notes
Form I-130 ✓ — — — Petition for Alien Relative. Petitioner signs. Beneficiary does not sign if outside US.
I-130A (Beneficiary Supplement) ✓ — — — Required for all spouse visas (CR1/IR1). Unsigned if completed outside the US.
Form I-864 (Affidavit of Support) — ✓ ✓ ✓ Wet-signed for primary sponsor (petitioner). Each sponsor completes separately.
I-864A (Joint Sponsor / Household Member) — * * * For any household member aged 18+ contributing income to support beneficiary.
Form DS-260 (Visa Application) — ✓ — — Online to NVC. All fields required. Prepare answers in advance (form times out).
DS-260 Confirmation Page — ✓ ✓ ✓ Printed from CEAC after submitting DS-260. Bring to medical exam and interview.
IV18a Questionnaire — — ✓ — Printed from CEAC. Bring to medical exam.

Interview Packet & Day-of Documents

Document I-130 NVC Medical Interview Notes
Interview Letter — — ✓ ✓ Printed from email. Bring to medical exam and interview.
Sealed Interview Packet — — ✓ ✓ From embassy document check. Contains passport photo + medical results. DO NOT OPEN before interview.
Pink Medical Checklist Slip — — ✓ ✓ Received from medical exam and taken to embassy. REQUIRED at interview. Proves medical was completed.
Any Requested Documents — — — * Any additional documents specifically requested by embassy in interview letter.
About this checklist: This consolidated master checklist combines requirements from all four stages (I-130, NVC, medical, and interview) organized by document type to eliminate duplication. Requirements vary by visa category and individual circumstances — always refer to the stage-specific checklists and official USCIS/State Department guidance for your specific case.
