3. If approved

Passport is retained for visa printing. You'll receive an email from the embassy with collection instructions.

Typical passport return: Mail Boxes Etc (MBE) — 2–3 days for pickup, 5–7 days for mail delivery.

See data tracker for current community averages on passport return times. Next: Life in the USA

4. If not approved

221(g) (Administrative Processing): Case under review. You'll receive an email with next steps. Most resolve within weeks to months.

Visa Pause: If you hold dual citizenship and one country is on a pause list, wait for DOS update.

Denial: Rare. Officer explains reason. Limited recourse; consult attorney.
