3. Document categories at I-130 stage

Category Required Notes
Civil (Identity) ✓ Birth certificates for both petitioner and beneficiary (certified copies). Petitioner must also prove US citizenship (passport, naturalization cert, or birth certificate).
Relationship ✓ Marriage certificate (certified copy). If previously married, all divorce decrees or death certificates (certified).
Translation ✓ All non-English documents must be translated. Certified translation required if USCIS deems it necessary (common for UK documents).
Forms ✓ I-130 (completed and signed by petitioner). I-130A if beneficiary is outside US.
Relationship Evidence ✓ (spouse/CR1/IR1) See Section 4 for spouse/CR1/IR1 evidence requirements. IR5/K visa categories have different requirements.
Medical — Medical exam not required at I-130 stage. Completed at later stages (NVC, Medical, Interview).
Police — Police certificates not required at I-130 stage. Required at NVC stage.

For a complete breakdown of all document requirements at each stage (I-130, NVC, Medical, Interview) with detailed notes on formats and translations, see Master Document Checklist.
